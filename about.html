Who we are

What we do

Arboreal is a subscription service for companies. The service means that you get your office decorated with plants chosen by our experts. You pay a monthly fee to get new plants delivered and placed in the office every month or week depending on the chosen price plan. Arboreal has its customer base among small to medium IT companies in the Stockholm area.

Mission

Arboreal revolutionizes the way you think about plants in an office environment. Arboreal isn't just a a delivery service for plants, it's a delivery service for new ideas. Arboreal makes it easy to keep your workplace fresh and oxygenated.
